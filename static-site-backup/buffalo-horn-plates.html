Buffalo Horn Plates

Buffalo Horn Plates, also known as Buffalo Scales, are widely used in crafting luxury horn eyewear. In addition, these plates are used in producing horn-handled knives such as the Aitor Jungle King, shaving handles, and other high-quality products.

The standard size of our Buffalo Horn Plates is: 60 mm x 160 mm, Model Number: BHP06.

The Moon Exports offers authentic, handcrafted buffalo horn plates made through meticulous crafting steps:

  • Hand-selecting premium quality Buffalo Horn specifically for plates.
  • Thoroughly washing and cleaning the buffalo horns.
  • Cutting the horns in half to prepare them for shaping.
  • Heating the cut horns at controlled temperatures to flatten them.
  • Giving them precise dimensions to ensure they fit perfectly in CNC machines without damage.
  • Cleaning the surface to remove any bumps or imperfections.
  • Refining the edges and eliminating lower-quality plates.

Prices are variable based on the following factors:

  • The quantity of the order
  • The thickness of the plate
  • The color mix of the horn

Feel free to contact us for a customized quote based on your specific requirements.

Once processed, the plates are sorted by color as follows:
Milky White Buffalo Horn Plates

The Milky White color in buffalo horn plates is the lightest shade, nearly white but slightly translucent. This color is rare, driving demand and higher prices. Considered an ultra-premium hue, it is highly sought after for bespoke projects.

Light White Buffalo Horn Plates

The Light White buffalo horn plates feature a lighter shade of brown with high transparency. This rare and premium color offers a refined look and is ideal for high-end projects.

Brown Buffalo Horn Plates

The Brown buffalo horn plates are highly popular for bespoke eyewear. These plates typically have fewer black streaks than the Dark Brown variety, which increases their demand for creating sophisticated horn products.

Dark Brown Buffalo Horn Plates

Dark Brown buffalo horn plates feature blackish tones with natural brown streaks. These plates provide a luxurious aesthetic, and the unique, organic striping ensures that each plate has its own distinct character.

Black Buffalo Horn Plates

Jet Black buffalo horn plates are a classic choice in horn eyewear. This deep black, opaque color is popular for its solid, bold appearance. Black plates are provided in their natural, unpolished state unless otherwise requested.

Black with White Buffalo Horn Plates

Black horn plates with white streaks are exceptionally rare and distinctive. These plates feature no transparency, maintaining their striking appearance in any lighting. Our Black with White horn plates are polished on one side to highlight the white streaks beautifully.

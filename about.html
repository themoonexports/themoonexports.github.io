The Moon Exports: Quality Buffalo Horn Handicrafts

Hello, I’m Kamran Khan, the founder of The Moon Exports. We specialize in delivering high-quality Buffalo Horn Products crafted with care and precision. Our team of skilled artisans uses traditional techniques and precision tools to create authentic Indian handicrafts.

The Moon Exports is a registered business entity located at Kot West, Sambhal – 244302, Uttar Pradesh, India. We are recognized by the Indian Director General of Foreign Trade and are proud members of the Export Promotion Council for Handicrafts.

The Moon Exports was established on 12th May 2015. I work closely with artisans to ensure that each piece is crafted to meet your exact needs. Our diverse product range includes Buffalo Horn Plates, Button Blanks, Finished Buttons, Stag Imitation Buttons, Bone Blanks, Horn Toggles, Buckles, Rollers, Scales/Plates for Spectacle Frames, and more.

Vision

Our vision is to promote authentic horn handicrafts by connecting customers directly with artisans who have spent decades honing their craft. We aim to make high-quality, handmade products accessible to all, whether in small quantities or bulk orders.

Mission

  • To create a trusted platform for ordering various handicrafts, including horn, wood, and resin crafts.
  • To promote Indian handicrafts on a global scale, with a focus on Buffalo Horn Crafts.
  • To support the #MadeinIndia initiative.

Affiliations

Artisan Alliance: We are committed to supporting the artisan sector, creating jobs, enhancing cultural heritage, and fostering development that respects the uniqueness of communities.

Federation of Indian Export Organisations (FIEO): FIEO is a trade promotion organization established in 1965 to support Indian exporters, in collaboration with the Ministry of Commerce and private industry.

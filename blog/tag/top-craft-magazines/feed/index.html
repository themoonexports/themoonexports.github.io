Here are top rated* magazines on crafts.

1) American Patchwork & Quilting Magazine

About American Patchwork & Quilting magazine

American Patchwork & Quilting magazine is perfect for quilters of all skill levels. Each issue contains easy-to-follow instructions, unique patterns, beautiful photos of finished quilts and helpful how-to articles. Whether you are a novice or an expert, you’ll glean helpful tips in each new issue of American Patchwork & Quilting magazine.

Each issue contains patterns for baby quilts, throws, table runners and more. The publication is filled with practical articles on how to cut, piece, embellish and more. Each issue teaches readers how to create bags, pillows, purses and gifts. American Patchwork & Quilting magazine also teaches readers how to make beautiful quilts for each holiday and season. Whether you are interested in hand quilting or machine quilting, American Patchwork & Quilting magazine will teach you everything you need to know.

American Patchwork & Quilting magazine is the perfect resource for anyone who wants to try their hand at sewing. Packed with quilting patterns, tips and instructions, it is the go-to guide for quilters everywhere.

Issues per Year: 6

Estimated Delivery: Up to 12 Weeks

Publishing Frequency: Bi-Monthly

Publisher Name: Meredith Corporation

2) Bead & Button Magazine

About Bead & Button

Bead & Button magazine shines a light on original ideas and the latest styles in the hobby of beading. Beading is a pastime for people who are in touch with their artsy side. Bead & Button provides step-by-step instructions for putting together necklaces, bracelets, pendants and more.

Readers can also improve their skills with the regular feature “Technique Workshop,” which explains different beading methods and styles. That includes: stringing, bead weaving, bead crochet, and wirework. Readers will learn from the experts and get a look inside their art studios in the “Artist Profile” section. Crafty people who want to make money from their homemade creations can also learn about selling and more in the regular feature, “The Business of Beading.” Plus, the bead creations of readers are regularly showcased in the “Your Work” section, which shines the spotlight on everyday beaders.

Anyone who loves DIY art projects with a particular passion for beading will love a subscription to Bead & Button magazine!

Issues per Year: 6

Estimated Delivery: Up to 12 Weeks

Publishing Frequency: Bi-Monthly

Publisher Name: Kalmbach Publishing Co.

3) Handwoven Magazine

About Handwoven

Handwoven is a weaver’s constant companion. Each issue offers a stunning collection of enticing projects. It’s a textile travelogue, pattern book and weave structure textbook. It’s a place to discover new patterns, learn to create original designs and find solutions to weaving challenges.

Issues per Year: 5

Estimated Delivery: 6-12 Weeks

Publishing Frequency: Seasonally

Publisher Name: F+W Media, Inc

4) Interweave Knits Magazine

About Interweave Knits

Interweave Knits is for knitters who fill their lives and dreams with knitting. Each issue features original designer patterns for garments and home decor, ranging from super-simple to challenging and from traditional to cutting edge.

Issues per Year: 4

Estimated Delivery: Up to 12 Weeks

Publishing Frequency: Quarterly

Publisher Name: F+W Media, Inc

5) Interweave Crochet Magazine

About Interweave Crochet

Interweave Crochet is your single best source for crochet news, ideas, articles and best of all – patterns! Prepare yourself for gasps of delight when your friends see you working on a project that goes way beyond the scarf and afghan.

Issues per Year: 4

Estimated Delivery: Up to 12 Weeks

Publishing Frequency: Quarterly

Publisher Name: F+W Media, Inc

6) Vogue Knitting International Magazine

About Vogue Knitting International

Relaunched in 1982, Vogue Knitting delivers topnotch needlework technique and high-end fashion to hand knitters around the globe. Published five times a year–spring, summer, early fall, fall, and winter–each issue offers up designer exclusives, the best knitwear from the industry’s top talents, and expert advice.

Content in the magazine is organized into two main sections: “Fashion” and “Features.” Popular columns in every issue include: “Spotlight,” a profile on a designer or knitting project; “Yarn Buying Guide,” tips for purchasing yarn; “Techniques and Abbreviations,” a glossary and guide to techniques and abbreviations in every issue; and “Fashion Resource,” where to find accessories and other non-knit pieces featured in the magazine. In addition, columns from needlework experts and multiple patterns and projects for several skill levels are included in every Vogue Knitting International issue.

Knitters looking for fashionable, elegant designs would find them in a subscription to Vogue Knitting International magazine!

Issues per Year: 5

Estimated Delivery: 6-12 Weeks

Publishing Frequency: Seasonally

Publisher Name: Soho Publishing Company

7) Creative Machine Embroidery Magazine

About Creative Machine Embroidery

Creative Machine Embroidery magazine is devoted to all things embroidery. Creative Machine Embroidery imparts machine embroidery enthusiasts of all ages and skill levels with the tools they need and the inspiration they crave to further their craft

Issues per Year: 4

Estimated Delivery: Up to 12 Weeks

Publishing Frequency: Quarterly

Publisher Name: F&W Media, Inc.

8) Watercolor Artist Magazine

About Watercolor Artist

Watercolor Artist is the perfect magazine for anyone who wants to learn more about painting with water-based paints. Each issue is brimming with artist profiles, step-by-step instructions and helpful hints. Beginning and experienced watercolor artists alike will glean useful information in each new issue of Watercolor Artist magazine.

Each issue of Watercolor Artist magazine contains watercolor techniques, tried-and-true tips and painting tricks. The magazine guides painters of all skill levels through basic and advanced techniques. Watercolor Artist also contains interviews with both up-and-coming artists and established painters alike. The publication prints full-color paintings of the artists’ work along with their interviews. Each issue also contains information about the best art materials, tools and strategies. Watercolor Artist highlights important information about upcoming contests and competitions and shows readers how to enter.

Watercolor Artist is the perfect companion for emerging and established painters alike. Each issue contains expert advice, in-depth tutorials and beautiful paintings. Watercolor enthusiasts will look forward to each new issue of Watercolor Artist!

Issues per Year: 6

Estimated Delivery: Up to 12 Weeks

Publishing Frequency: Bi-Monthly

Publisher Name: F & W Media, Inc

9) Rock & Gem Magazine

About Rock & Gem

Rock & Gem magazine is the ultimate authority on rocks and minerals. Each issue is filled with information about collecting trends, minerals and classic deposits. Rockhounds of all ages will appreciate a subscription to Rock & Gem.

Each issue is filled with articles on mineral collecting, gold prospecting, jewelry making and more. Founded by Carnegie Award winner Bob Jones, the publication is written by a panel of knowledgeable experts. The experts share their insights on lapidary techniques, faceting and the art of mineral collecting. In each issue, field trip writers teach readers how to find the perfect collecting spots and collect the most beautiful gems. Each issue is filled with step-by-step projects for lapidaries of all skill levels. The easy-to-follow instructions will have you tumbling, polishing and making jewelry in no time. The magazine also contains behind-the-scenes coverage of the year’s biggest and best mineral shows.

Hobbyists and diehard diggers alike will look forward to each new issue of Rock & Gem magazine.

Issues per Year: 12

Estimated Delivery: 6-8 Weeks

Publishing Frequency: Monthly

Publisher Name: Beckett Collectibles, Inc

10) Threads Magazine

About Threads

Threads magazine was created for individuals who are passionate about sewing. Each bimonthly issue is filled with tips, techniques, insights and ideas. Sewers of all skill levels will glean practical, hands-on advice in each new issue of Threads magazine.

Each issue of Threads contains dozens of patterns for gifts, accessories, home furnishings and clothes. Whether you want to create a handmade skirt or sew a beautiful rug, you’ll receive easy-to-follow instructions and step-by-step photos in each new issue. The patterns range in level from easy to difficult, so you can easily find one regardless of your previous experience. Each issue of Threads also covers a variety of topics like design fitting, finishing and embellishment. The publication also explains the basic fundamentals of sewing. Individuals who want to teach themselves to sew will receive expert advice in each new issue.

Threads magazine is the perfect choice for sewers of all ages. Each issue is filled with creative patterns, original ideas and helpful suggestions. Sewing enthusiasts everywhere will look forward to each new issue of Threads magazine.

Issues per Year: 6

Estimated Delivery: Up to 12 Weeks

Publishing Frequency: Bi-Monthly

Publisher Name: Taunton Direct
